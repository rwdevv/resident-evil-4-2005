“Resident Evil 4”, released in 2005, is a milestone in Capcom's survival horror series. The game follows agent Leon S. Kennedy on a mission to rescue the US president's daughter, captured by a cult in rural Europe. With an intensely tense atmosphere and set in gloomy villages and castles, it offers an immersive experience with an over-the-shoulder perspective. Featuring new gameplay mechanics and balancing horror and action, the game is acclaimed for its immersive narrative and detailed level design.
